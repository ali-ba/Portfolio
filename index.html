About Me

I am a Front End Developer based in Auckland, New Zealand. I specialise in web design using HTML5, CSS/SASS and Javascript.
Two years into my computer science degree at the University of Auckland I realised my interests and talent are better suited to web development. I changed my study path to Web and UX Design and enrolled at Yoobee Design School. My skills, knowledge and passion for web design has grown over the last year and I am proficient in many web languages and frameworks.
